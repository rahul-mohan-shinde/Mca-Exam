# Research Process and Its Steps

## Definition

The **Research Process** is a systematic sequence of steps followed to solve a research problem and achieve research objectives.

---

# Steps in Research Process

### 1. Identify the Research Problem

* Select and define the problem to be studied.
* Example: "Why are students scoring low in Mathematics?"

### 2. Review Literature

* Study books, journals, and previous research related to the topic.
* Helps understand what has already been researched.

### 3. Set Objectives and Hypothesis

* Define what the research aims to achieve.
* Form a hypothesis (a tentative assumption) if required.

### 4. Research Design

* Plan how the research will be conducted.
* Decide methods, tools, sample size, and data sources.

### 5. Data Collection

* Gather information from primary or secondary sources.
* Methods: Surveys, questionnaires, interviews, observations.

### 6. Data Analysis

* Organize and analyze collected data.
* Use statistical tools, charts, and graphs.

### 7. Interpretation of Results

* Explain the meaning of analyzed data.
* Draw conclusions from the findings.

### 8. Report Writing and Presentation

* Prepare the final research report.
* Present findings, conclusions, and recommendations.

---

# Flow of Research Process

```text
Research Problem
        ↓
Literature Review
        ↓
Objectives/Hypothesis
        ↓
Research Design
        ↓
Data Collection
        ↓
Data Analysis
        ↓
Interpretation
        ↓
Report Writing
```

# Short Exam Answer (5 Marks)

The research process is a systematic method of conducting research. It includes identifying the problem, reviewing literature, setting objectives, designing the research, collecting data, analyzing data, interpreting results, and preparing the final report.
