# Research Question

## Definition

A **Research Question** is a clear, focused, and specific question that a researcher wants to answer through research.

It guides the entire research process and helps determine what data needs to be collected and analyzed.

---

## Examples of Research Questions

### Example 1 (Education)

**Research Question:**

* How does social media usage affect students' academic performance?

### Example 2 (Business)

**Research Question:**

* What factors influence customer satisfaction in online shopping?

### Example 3 (Health)

**Research Question:**

* Does regular exercise improve mental health among college students?

### Example 4 (Technology)

**Research Question:**

* How does artificial intelligence impact workplace productivity?

---

## Characteristics of a Good Research Question

1. Clear and specific.
2. Researchable and answerable.
3. Relevant to the topic.
4. Focused and not too broad.
5. Helps achieve research objectives.

---

## Short Exam Answer (2–3 Marks)

A **Research Question** is a specific question that a researcher aims to answer through systematic investigation. It provides direction to the research study.

**Example:** "How does social media usage affect students' academic performance?"
