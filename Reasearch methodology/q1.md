# Research – Definition

**Research** is a systematic and scientific process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting information to discover new facts or solve a problem.

**Simple English:**
Research means finding answers to questions through careful study and investigation.

---

# Importance of Research

1. Helps in solving problems.
2. Provides new knowledge and information.
3. Supports decision-making.
4. Improves existing methods and processes.
5. Helps in business, science, education, and technology development.
6. Reduces uncertainty and risk.

**Short Answer:**
Research is important because it helps generate knowledge, solve problems, and make better decisions.

---

# Objectives of Research

1. To discover new facts and knowledge.
2. To verify existing theories and concepts.
3. To identify and solve problems.
4. To understand relationships between variables.
5. To develop new techniques, products, or methods.
6. To support planning and decision-making.

**Short Exam Answer:**
The main objectives of research are to gain knowledge, test theories, solve problems, and support decision-making.
